Coffee Sponge Roll

Instructions

  1. Add the vegetable oil and hot black coffee (85g) into a bowl, then mix until combined

  2. Siv the cake flour into the oil and coffee mixture, then fold until mixed.

  3. Seperate the egg white and yolks. KEEP THE EGG WHITES

  4. Add the 4 yolks into coffee mixture, then mixed until smooth.

    5. Cream

  5. Mix the egg white until puffed up (like milk), then add lemon juice and in ALL of the sugar.

  6. Mix the egg white until thickened. You should be able to make a hook by pulling the blender upwards and away from the mixture.

  7. Mix together the egg white and the coffee mixture, until it's fully mixed.

    8. Assemble

  8. Preheat your oven to 150¬∞c

  9. Pour the egg white and coffee mixture into a flat tray, then bake for 30 minutes.

  10. After you take it out, flip it over so the side with the parchment paper on is facing the ceiling

  11. On the side you just peeled of the parchment paper, put a new piece of parchment paper on

  12. Wait until the cake has cooled down, only then you peel off the parchment paper. If the skin of the cake comes off, it's fine because it is the inner side of the cake.

  13. Make sure you have parchment paper on the undamaged side of the cake base as well.

  14. Equally spread the whipped cream across the cake base, with 1/3 of the cake base having a thicker layer of whipped (coffee) cream.

  15. Roll up the cake base + whipped cream, then refrigerate for at least 1 hour before eating

  16. Enjoy!
