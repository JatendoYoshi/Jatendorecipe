Instructions

  1. Seperate the egg yolks and the egg whites *Put the egg whites in a big bowl*

  2. Pour in the milk and vegetable oil into a big bowl *Not the one you put the egg whites in, you will need a seperate one*, then whisk together for about 2-3 minutes

  3. Add in the Matcha Powder, then whisk again until its mixed

  4. Siv the half of the flour in, then whisk lightly until mixed

  5. Siv the remaining half of the flour in, then whisk lightly until mixed

  6. Add in the egg yolks, the mixture should be slightly watery at this point

  7. Preheat your oven to 150¬∞c

  8. Squeeze in the Lemon juice into your bowl of egg white, then mix together with an electric mixer on medium-high speed until puffy

  9. Add in 1/3 of the caster sugar, then mix together on high speed

  10. Add in 1/3 of the caster sugar, then mix together on medium speed, the mixture should be able to almost stand up now

  11. Add in the rest of the caster sugar, then mix together on low speed, the mixture should stand up like a curve now

  12. Add in 1/3 of the egg white mixture into your matcha mixture, then whisk together lightly with a normal whisk

  13. Add in your mixture of egg white and matcha into the bowl of egg white, then whisk together lightly. It should look like a light green colour now

  14. Prepare a 25x38x2cm tray, then put a piece of parchment paper on it to keep the cake smooth

  15. Pour the mixture into the tray, then flatten it up so it's not bumpy

  16. Put the tray of mixture in the preheated oven for 20-22 minutes

  17. Then bake at 165¬∞c for 6 minutes

  18. After you take it out, flip it over so the side with the parchment paper on is facing the ceiling

  19. On the side you just peeled of the parchment paper, put a new piece of parchment paper on

  20. Wait until the cake has cooled down, only then you peeled off the parchment paper. If the skin of the cake comes off, it's fine because it is the inner side of the cake.

  21. Make sure you have parchment paper on the undamaged side of the cake base as well.

  22. Put Thickened Cream, Icing sugar and matcha powder into a bowl, then mix together on medium speed first, then change to low speed after a while

  23. The cream should be thick now. Spread the cream across the cake base evenly, that will be our cream filling

  24. Roll it up carefully, and then the cake is finished!

  25. Enjoy!
